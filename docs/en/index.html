Community Centre in Lielauce

Community Centre in Lielauce

Renovation of Lielauce Community Centre in „Ezerkrasti”, Auce district. The object has state cultural monument status. Completed works: renovation of the roof, replacement of windows and doors, installation of new floor decking, new electric installations, renovation of the facade. Realisation of the project - year 2010. Object area - 730m2. Contracting authority: Local authority of Auce district.
